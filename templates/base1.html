D.Pharma

Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharma) is a short-term undergraduate full-time Diploma Course of Pharmacy at Aryans Pharmacy College that lasts for two years. D.Pharma is registered under Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi and affiliated with PSBTE (Punjab State Board of Technical Education). It becomes easier when it comes to studying this course in India as students can consider this course right after completing 12th in science stream with the subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology or Mathematics. After the completion of this course, it is mandatory for every candidate to complete respective Pharmacy Council Registration to work as a Pharmacist. This programme helps the students to learn about various chemical salts, their applications, and uses in the medicines. This programme holds good value in the pharmaceutical industry.
